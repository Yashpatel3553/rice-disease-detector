Detectable Diseases

Our model identifies 4 major rice leaf diseases from the Mendeley dataset

🦠
Bacterial Blight
Water-soaked lesions along leaf edges turning yellow then white-grey. Caused by Xanthomonas oryzae. 20–30% yield loss if untreated.
High Risk
💨
Blast
Diamond-shaped lesions with grey centres and brown margins. Caused by Magnaporthe oryzae fungus. Most damaging rice disease globally.
High Risk
🟤
Brown Spot
Circular to oval brown spots with yellow halo. Caused by Cochliobolus miyabeanus. Common in nutrient-poor, drought-stressed soils.
Medium Risk
🌀
Tungro
Yellow-orange discolouration, stunted growth. Viral disease spread by green leafhoppers. Causes severe tillering reduction.
High Risk

About This Model
